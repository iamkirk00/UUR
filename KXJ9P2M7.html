Marine Turtles

The project focuses on monitoring the population of sea turtles using advanced ROV technology.

ROV Technology

ROV systems utilize autonomous underwater vehicles to collect data in remote areas.

Key Components

  • Autonomous Navigation Systems
  • Environmental Sensors
  • Real-time Data Transmission

Data Collection

ROV vehicles collect data on marine life, water quality, and environmental factors.

Parameter Unit Range
Depth Meters 0-1000

Deployment Strategy

The ROV vehicles will be deployed in key marine areas to gather data over a 6-month period.

Key Deployment Areas

  • Gulf of Mexico
  • Pacific Ocean
  • Indian Ocean
